Website UX & Conversion Audit

Based on a real-time audit of attorneyguss.com/chicago, these key findings highlight visual friction, conversion gaps, and user experience opportunities.

Hero Section Weak CTA

The homepage opens with: "We will treat your case like it matters, like you matter." While emotionally resonant, there's no visible button or action prompt above the fold. Visitors may not know what to do next.

Recommendation: Add a high-contrast "Free Case Review" button beside or below the headline.

Mobile Header UX Gaps

On smaller screens, the phone number and navigation are cramped. The menu icon overlaps with the logo, reducing tap area.

Recommendation: Add breathing room to header, make phone tap target larger and sticky.

Lack of Instant Trust Signals

Key credibility markers — awards, case results, and testimonials — are hidden mid-scroll or on subpages. They don't appear upfront.

Recommendation: Show "Featured In", "Client Wins", and "Google Rating" badges in top section.

Missing Sticky Contact Options

The website offers a contact form and phone number, but they're only visible after scrolling. On mobile, there's no floating call/chat option.

Recommendation: Add a sticky "Call Now" or "Message Us" bar for constant availability.
